Notice

BeautyBar is not yet fully working product, so one should be aware that all data can be removed without notice, and also lots of other development with ui, internal logics, etc. will happend.

Purpose

Purpose of BeautyBar is to make one able to produce visually attractive bar graphs in a simple way. These graphs can then be used to make presentations and documents look more professional and nicer. Let's not use always those old plain bar graphs in presentations!
